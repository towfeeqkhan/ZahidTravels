Top Places To Visit

KASHMIR VALLEY

Paradise on Earth

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir Valley, where pristine lakes mirror snow-capped peaks, and traditional houseboats float on serene waters. Explore the magnificent Mughal gardens, take a shikara ride on Dal Lake, and immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of this heavenly destination.

Explore More

GULMARG

Winter Wonderland

Discover Gulmarg, India's premier ski destination and a summer paradise. Take the world's highest gondola ride, ski down pristine slopes in winter, or enjoy golf and trekking during summer. With its meadows of wildflowers and panoramic Himalayan views, Gulmarg offers year-round adventures.

Explore More

PAHALGAM

Valley of Shepherds

Visit Pahalgam, where the Lidder River flows through pine forests and mountain meadows. Perfect for river rafting, trekking, and fishing, this scenic valley offers a peaceful retreat. Explore ancient temples, go horseback riding, or simply relax amid nature's grandeur.
